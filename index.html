Laser System for Plant Dissection

I designed and Prototyped an automated laser cutting system to dissect plant tissue, using a 20w co2 laser and a 3D stage. It had to be safe for the user and have precision on the micron scale. The system also has to be temprture regulated and uv sterialized to keep the plant wounds from infection and damage. (click to see the report)

Magic Slide Whistle

The main goal of this project is for the RPi to control a slide whistle as a musical instrument and play songs. A stepper motor will drive a linear gear rail that will control the slide of the whistle. The system is programmed so that it will automatically move the slide to the correct location to produce notes of a song. This system will remind the human “musician” when they should play our autonomous instrument, resulting in a Magical Slide Whistle that “plays itself”

Guppy Hunters

Our team chose to create an arcade-style game that we have called “Guppy Hunters.” The motivation behind it was to utilize the flocking behavior of boids, to model the behavior of a school of fish working together to avoid a predator. In this project we implemented hardware components, such as joysticks and push buttons as well as software components such as modifying the existing boids algorithms and creating game states.

Distance Measurement with White Light Interferometry

This project used white light interferometry to measure cavity lengths. Length measurements with small uncertainty are required in the field of nanotechnology and ultra precision engineering. We will start with a Fabry-Perot cavity made by a fiber and a mirror. We will take into account phase changes with frequency with Bragg's coating on the tip of the fiber and the phase change of the mirror.
